Our Story

Intelligence that
moves at market speed

IntellScout was built for one reason: the gap between data and decision is too costly. We bridge it — pulling live competitive signals from across the web, structuring them into actionable reports within seconds, so your team always plays with full information.

◈
Live Intelligence
We don't use stale databases. Every analysis pulls fresh data from the open web via Bright Data's enterprise-grade scraping infrastructure — news, pricing pages, press releases, and more.
◉
AI-Structured Reports
Raw data is noise. Our AI layer synthesizes live results into structured GTM reports — company summaries, pricing signals, expansion moves, partnership alerts, and risk assessments.
◇
No-Code Workflow
The entire pipeline runs on n8n — meaning the workflow is fully transparent, modifiable, and yours to own. No black-box SaaS subscriptions. Total control over your intelligence stack.
⬡
Built for Speed
From query submission to structured report in under 30 seconds. IntellScout is designed for teams who need answers before their next meeting — not their next quarterly review.
